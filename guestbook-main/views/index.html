Data Protection and Privacy

 

Data protection and privacy are important to us, which is why we want to ensure transparency. Your consent to use the IBM Guestbook demo is absolutely freely and voluntarily. The uploaded photos are stored and processed only temporarily for the duration of the product demonstration on the IBM Cloud Servers. A further storage and data processing of these data (Name and Photo) does not take place either.
